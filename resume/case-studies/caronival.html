• About This Project

Have you ever found yourself visiting a new city unsure of what to explore? Your first thought, likely, is to use Yelp or TripAdvisor to find restaurants to eat at or landmarks to visit. But those apps are for tourists... Why not live like a local? Caronival pulls from local events based on your location (entered or GPS) and can even direct you there.
