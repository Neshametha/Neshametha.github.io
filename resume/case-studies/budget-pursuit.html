Going out on your own is tough. Finances are not typically taught in the gradeschool years or even college years for most students. But what if there was a way to make it fun? Budget Pursuit is the perfect way to gameify your finances. Create your character and level them up based on your financial goals!

  • User Research & Target Audience
User Information
Who is the ideal user?
  • Preferably a senior in high school or older.
  • User is coming into adulthood that is managing their finances on their own for the first time, perhaps even planning for the future.
  • Is into video games, especially roleplaying games (RPGs).
User Scenario
When and how will the user utilize this app?
  • Equipment: Mobile phone (iOS or Android)
  • Time: Whenever
  • Location: Wherever
  • Internet Connection: Required
  • Potential Distractions: Other money-saving apps such as Rocket Money, Acorns, or Cleo.
Motivations
Why would the user use this app?
  • Wants to learn how to properly manage their finances in a way that is easy to grasp but is also fun.
  • User could be looking to make a big purchase such as a home or a vehicle and would like help saving money every month until they are ready.
What would motivate the user to continue using this app?
  • If user sees financial growth, they may continue using the app to keep saving more and more money.
  • To continue leveling up their RPG character.
Constraints
What would be the reason(s) the user stops using or uninstalls the app?
  • User is not seeing any results; their finances feel the same.
  • The RPG aspect stops being fun or is not fun for them from the get-go.
