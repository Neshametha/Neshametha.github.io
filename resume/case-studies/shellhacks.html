A hackathon is a weekend-long event where students come together to learn about the latest technologies and build innovative products, and ShellHacks is no exception. ShellHacks, also known as Florida’s Largest Hackathon, had a Miami Beach theme in 2019 that also incorporated its signature colors, pink and teal. For students to sign up for the hackathon, we needed a page that suit the theme and was able to contain information, frequently asked questions, and links to register. As the Co-Director of the hackathon, I wanted us to go all-out on the theme.
